# Privacy Policy for Plantdoku

**Effective date:** 27 June 2026
**Last updated:** 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game **Plantdoku** ("the App", "we",
"us", "our") handles information when you use it. Plantdoku is a single‑player
logic puzzle game. We have tried to collect as little as possible: there are
**no accounts, no sign‑in, no ads, and no in‑app purchases**, and we do not ask
you for your name, email, phone number, or any other directly identifying
information.

## 1. Summary (the short version)

- We **do not** require an account and **do not** collect your name, email, or
  contacts.
- Your **game progress** (levels, best times, stars, streaks) is stored **only
  on your device** and is never uploaded to us.
- We use **PostHog**, a privacy‑friendly analytics service, to collect
  **anonymous, aggregated usage data** (e.g. which level you finished and how
  long it took) so we can improve the game.
- Analytics data is associated with a **random, app‑generated ID** — not with
  you personally, and **not** with your device's advertising identifier (IDFA /
  AAID). We **do not track you across other apps or websites.**
- Analytics data is processed by PostHog on servers in the **European Union**.

---

## 2. Information we process

### 2.1 Stored only on your device (not collected by us)

The following is saved locally on your device using standard app storage and is
**never transmitted to us**. Deleting the app removes it:

- Level progress and which levels are unlocked
- Best completion times (levels, daily puzzle, endless mode)
- Star ratings and unlocked plant cards
- Daily‑puzzle streak and history
- Settings and whether you have completed the tutorial

### 2.2 Anonymous analytics (collected via PostHog)

To understand how the game is played and where players get stuck, we send
anonymous event data to our analytics provider, **PostHog**. This includes:

- **Gameplay events** — e.g. a level started, a level completed (with the time
  taken, difficulty, hints used, and lives remaining), a puzzle failed, hints
  requested, plant cards unlocked, and which screen you are viewing.
- **A random analytics identifier** generated by the app on first launch. It is
  not your name, account, or advertising ID. You can reset it at any time (see
  Section 6).
- **Basic device and app information** automatically included by the analytics
  SDK: device model and type, operating system and version, app version and
  build, language/locale, and time zone.
- **Approximate location (country/region only)**, derived by PostHog from your
  IP address at the time of connection. We do **not** collect GPS or precise
  location.

We do **not** collect: your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos,
precise (GPS) location, health data, financial information, or your device's
advertising identifier.

---

## 3. How we use information

We use the information described above only to:

- Operate the game and save your progress on your device;
- Understand aggregate usage (e.g. completion rates, difficulty, drop‑off
  points) to fix bugs and improve level design and balance;
- Maintain the security and stability of the App.

We do **not** use your information for advertising, profiling, or automated
decisions that produce legal effects, and we do **not** sell or rent it.

## 4. Sharing and third parties

We do not sell your information. We share data only with the service that helps
us run the App:

| Provider    | Purpose                | Data processed                              | More info |
|-------------|------------------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------|
| **PostHog** | Product analytics      | Anonymous events, random ID, device info, IP‑derived region | https://posthog.com/privacy |

PostHog acts as our **data processor** and processes the analytics data on our
behalf on servers located in the **European Union**. We may also disclose
information if required to do so by law.

## 5. Data retention

- **On‑device data** remains until you delete it (via the in‑game "reset/flush
  data" option) or uninstall the App.
- **Analytics data** is retained by PostHog for a limited period for trend
  analysis and then deleted or aggregated. You may request deletion at any time
  (Section 6).

## 6. Your choices and rights

- **Reset your data:** Use the in‑game Settings → flush/reset data option to
  erase all on‑device progress and start fresh. This also resets your anonymous
  analytics identity.
- **Stop analytics:** _(If/when an in‑app analytics opt‑out toggle is added,
  describe it here.)_
- Depending on where you live, you may have rights to **access, correct,
  delete, or restrict** the processing of your data, and to **object** to it or
  request **portability**. Because our analytics data is anonymous and not tied
  to your identity, we may be unable to locate data about a specific individual;
  resetting your data in‑app is the most reliable way to clear it. To make a
  request, email **benmithat18@gmail.com**.

### EEA/UK (GDPR)
Our legal basis for processing anonymous analytics is our **legitimate interest**
in understanding and improving the game. You may object to this processing as
described above.

### California (CCPA/CPRA)
We do **not** sell or "share" (for cross‑context behavioral advertising) your
personal information, and we do not collect the categories of sensitive personal
information used for such purposes.

## 7. Children's privacy

Plantdoku is suitable for general audiences but is **not directed to children
under the age of 13** (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has
provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

> ⚠️ **Note:** If you choose to publish Plantdoku in a "Kids" / "Made for
> Children" / "Designed for Families" category, the third‑party analytics
> described here may not be permitted without additional safeguards. Revisit
> this policy and your analytics configuration before doing so.

## 8. Security

We use industry‑standard measures to protect data, including encryption in
transit (HTTPS/TLS) for all analytics traffic. No method of transmission or
storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.
